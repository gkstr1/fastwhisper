# Personal life management — ideas (Max as PA)

## Planning + execution
- Daily/weekly planning: “Top 3” priorities + time blocks.
- Habit tracker: sleep, workouts, reading, hydration.
- Recurring routines: Sunday reset checklist.
- Decision log: big choices + why.

## Calendar + time
- Create events, holds, reminders, prep notes.
- Travel buffers + commute time blocks.
- Meeting prep packets: agenda, links, last notes, next actions.
- Birthday/anniversary reminders.

## Email + messages (approval-based)
- Inbox triage: urgent/important, newsletters, receipts.
- Draft replies (never send without explicit approval).
- Follow-up nudges: “You said you’d reply to X.”

## Health
- Appointment scheduling + reminders.
- Medication/supplement reminders.
- Symptom log + questions for doctor.
- Meal planning and grocery list.

## Home + errands
- Errand batching list (by neighborhood/time).
- House “ops”: cleaning schedule, supplies restock.
- Vendor management: plumber/electrician/cleaner contact list.

## Money (spend carefully)
- Subscription audit + cancel list.
- Monthly budget snapshot + alerts.
- Receipts tracking + categorized notes.

## Relationships
- Keep-in-touch prompts (friends/family cadence).
- Gift ideas list per person.
- Event planning: dinners, trips, meetups.

## Learning + projects
- Reading list + summaries.
- Skill plan: weekly learning goals.
- Project tracker for personal goals.

## Travel
- Trip planning: itinerary, packing list, confirmations.
- Price watch (flights/hotels) and reminders.

## “Life admin” vault
- Central doc with: IDs, account list, emergency contacts (secure).
- Insurance policy summary.

## Safety / guardrails
- Max can draft, plan, organize, remind.
- Max never spends money, books travel, or sends messages without approval.
